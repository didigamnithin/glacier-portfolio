# Social Media Strategy — Glacier Portfolio (nithin)

## 1. Product Name & Category
**Product:** Glacier Portfolio
**Category:** Personal Branding & Project Showcase (Web/Creative)

## 2. Target Audience
**Who are we selling to?**
- Potential clients and employers in the tech and AI space.
- Collaborators and fellow developers.
- The "Build in Public" community looking for inspiration.

## 3. Primary Distribution Channels
- **LinkedIn:** Building a professional brand and sharing project milestones.
- **Twitter/X:** Engaging with the tech community and sharing "Build in Public" updates.
- **Personal Website:** The central hub for all project details and contact info.
- **GitHub:** Showcasing the code behind the portfolio and featured projects.

## 4. Content Strategy
- **Project Deep Dives:** Detailed posts on the "Why" and "How" behind major AI projects.
- **Building in Public Updates:** Regular shares on the journey of creating new tools and products.
- **Tech Stack Showcases:** Discussing the tools and frameworks used in different projects.
- **Founder Insights:** Sharing personal philosophies on tech, AI, and entrepreneurship.

## 5. Growth Hack / Viral Loop
**The "Client Referral" Loop:**
Implement a clear "Refer a Client" or "Work with Me" section that offers a commission or bonus for successful project referrals. This turns visitors and collaborators into a distributed sales force for my personal brand and services.
